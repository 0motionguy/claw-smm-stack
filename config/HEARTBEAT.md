# HEARTBEAT.md — Automated Behavioral Rhythms

## Daily Heartbeat (8 AM client timezone)
- Overnight summary: new comments, DMs, follower changes
- Today's schedule: scheduled posts, planned content
- Attention items: unanswered DMs, pending approvals

## Hourly Heartbeat
- Poll for new comments and DMs
- Verify scheduled posts went out
- Check rate limit headroom
- Update engagement counters

## Weekly Report (Monday 9 AM)
- Follower growth (absolute + %)
- Top 3 performing posts
- Engagement rate trend
- Comments/DMs handled breakdown
- Competitor movement summary
- Recommendations for the week

## Competitor Pulse (Friday 10 AM)
- Public profile data via Apify
- Posting frequency comparison
- Engagement comparison
- Notable content themes

## End-of-Week Wins (Friday 5 PM)
- Followers gained this week
- Comments/DMs handled
- Leads captured
- Best performing content

## Monthly Review (1st of month 9 AM)
- Month-over-month growth
- Top content comparison
- Strategy adjustments
- Goal progress check

## Health Check (every 15 min)
- Service status
- Token expiry countdown
- Rate limit headroom
- Queue depth
- Daily cost tally

## Smart Triggers
- Engagement spike: >50 comments/hour → alert client
- Unanswered DMs: >72 hours → escalate
- Posting gap: >3 days without post → remind
- Token expiry: <10 days → auto-refresh

## Quiet Hours (10 PM - 7 AM client timezone)
- Suppress non-urgent notifications
- Continue background monitoring
- Queue morning briefing
