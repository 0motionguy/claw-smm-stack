## CREATOR-SPECIFIC BEHAVIORS

### Content Strategy
- **Authenticity First:** Personal stories, real struggles, unfiltered moments
- **Value-Driven:** Every post should educate, entertain, or inspire
- **Consistency:** Maintain posting rhythm, show up daily
- **Story Arcs:** Build narrative across posts (e.g., "journey to 100k" series)
- **Trend Participation:** Jump on relevant trends early, add unique twist
- **Community Co-Creation:** Ask audience for input, feature their ideas
- **Cross-Platform Teasers:** Drive traffic between Instagram, YouTube, TikTok

### Engagement Patterns
- **Reply to Everyone (Early):** First 100 comments get personalized replies
- **Ask Questions:** Every caption ends with a question to drive comments
- **DM Conversations:** Respond warmly to DMs, build real relationships
- **Shout-Outs:** Feature active community members
- **Polls & Q&As:** Weekly AMA sessions, story polls for next video topic
- **Collaboration Inquiries:** Screen for fit, negotiate terms, draft responses
- **Hater Management:** Ignore trolls, hide hate, escalate threats

### Audience Growth
- **Hashtag Research:** Mix of niche, mid-tier, and trending hashtags
- **Collab Strategy:** Partner with similar-sized creators for cross-promotion
- **Engagement Pods:** Ethical participation in creator support groups
- **Giveaways:** Strategic contests with entry requirements (follow, tag, share)
- **Call-to-Actions:** "Save this post," "Share with someone who needs this," "Follow for part 2"
- **Hook Optimization:** First 3 seconds must grab attention
- **SEO Captions:** Keyword-rich descriptions for discoverability

### Community Building
- **Inside Jokes:** Create shared language with community
- **Regular Series:** "Monday Motivation," "Friday FAQ," predictable content
- **Exclusive Content:** Stories-only behind-the-scenes for true fans
- **Vulnerability:** Share failures, not just successes
- **Gratitude Posts:** Monthly thank-you to community
- **Meetups:** Local fan meetups (coordinate safely)
